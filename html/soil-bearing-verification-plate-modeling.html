    Landasan Teori Perhitungan Theoretical Basis of Calculation

    Analisis ini bertujuan untuk memastikan keamanan dan stabilitas platform kerja untuk operasional alat berat, baik secara langsung di atas tanah maupun dengan menggunakan perantara steel plate (matras). Platform kerja harus mampu menahan beban yang diberikan tanpa mengalami keruntuhan atau deformasi berlebih [1, 4]. Metodologi perhitungan mengacu pada panduan desain platform kerja oleh EFFC/DFI dan praktik umum rekayasa geoteknik dan struktural. Verifikasi mencakup tiga aspek utama: This analysis aims to ensure the safety and stability of the working platform for heavy equipment operations, either directly on the ground or using steel plates (mats). The working platform must be capable of supporting the applied loads without failure or excessive deformation [1, 4]. The calculation methodology refers to the working platform design guide by EFFC/DFI and common geotechnical and structural engineering practices. The verification covers three main aspects:

    1. Verifikasi Daya Dukung Tanah (Ground Bearing Pressure) 1. Ground Bearing Pressure Verification

    Tujuan verifikasi ini adalah untuk memastikan bahwa tekanan yang diberikan oleh alat berat ke tanah ($q_{\text{demand}}$) tidak melampaui kapasitas aman tanah ($q_{\text{allowable}}$). Kapasitas aman diperoleh dari data uji tanah ($q_{\text{allow,test}}$), seperti uji CBR atau Plate Bearing Test, yang kemudian direduksi dengan Faktor Keamanan (FoS) untuk mengantisipasi ketidakpastian kondisi tanah dan variasi material [1]. The objective of this verification is to ensure that the pressure exerted by the heavy equipment on the ground ($q_{\text{demand}}$) does not exceed the soil's safe capacity ($q_{\text{allowable}}$). The safe capacity is obtained from soil test data ($q_{\text{allow,test}}$), such as CBR or Plate Bearing Tests, which is then reduced by a Factor of Safety (FoS) to account for uncertainties in soil conditions and material variations [1].

    $q_{\text{allowable}} = \frac{q_{\text{allow,test}}}{\text{FoS}}$

    Tekanan aktual yang terjadi ($q_{\text{demand}}$) dihitung dengan memperhitungkan berat operasi total alat ($W$) yang dikalikan dengan faktor beban ($\gamma_F$). Faktor ini mengakomodasi efek dinamis, beban eksentris, dan intensitas operasional. Panduan EFFC/DFI merekomendasikan nilai $\gamma_F$ antara 1.1 hingga 1.2, tergantung pada tingkat risiko dan kondisi operasional [4]. Beban berfaktor ini kemudian didistribusikan ke seluruh area kontak. The actual pressure ($q_{\text{demand}}$) is calculated considering the total operating weight of the equipment ($W$) multiplied by a load factor ($\gamma_F$). This factor accommodates dynamic effects, eccentric loads, and operational intensity. The EFFC/DFI guide recommends a $\gamma_F$ value between 1.1 and 1.2, depending on the risk level and operating conditions [4]. This factored load is then distributed over the contact area.

    Tekanan Aktual (dengan plate): $q_{\text{demand}} = \frac{\gamma_F \times W \times g}{2 \times L_{\text{plate}} \times b_{\text{plate}}}$ Actual Pressure (with plate): $q_{\text{demand}} = \frac{\gamma_F \times W \times g}{2 \times L_{\text{plate}} \times b_{\text{plate}}}$

    Syarat Aman: $q_{\text{demand}} \le q_{\text{allowable}}$ Safety Condition: $q_{\text{demand}} \le q_{\text{allowable}}$

    2. Verifikasi Struktural Steel Plate 2. Structural Verification of Steel Plate

    Ketika steel plate digunakan, integritas struktural plate itu sendiri harus diverifikasi terhadap dua mode kegagalan utama: lentur (bending) dan geser pons (punching shear) [1, 4]. When a steel plate is used, its structural integrity must be verified against two primary failure modes: bending and punching shear [1, 4].

    a. Verifikasi Lentur (Bending Stress) a. Bending Stress Verification

    Model analisis mengasumsikan bagian plate yang menjorok keluar dari area kontak track (cantilever) menerima tekanan balik dari tanah ($q_{\text{demand}}$). Tekanan ini menyebabkan momen lentur pada plate. Tegangan lentur aktual ($\sigma_{\text{bending}}$) dihitung menggunakan formula standar untuk plat kantilever dengan beban merata [2]. Tegangan ini tidak boleh melebihi tegangan izin material ($\sigma_{\text{allowable}}$). Berdasarkan standar desain baja seperti AISC 360, tegangan izin untuk kondisi layan seringkali diambil sebagai persentase dari kuat leleh baja ($f_y$). Nilai yang umum digunakan untuk desain perancah atau struktur temporer adalah $0.75 \times f_y$, yang memberikan faktor keamanan tambahan [3]. The analysis model assumes the portion of the plate cantilevering beyond the track contact area is subjected to upward pressure from the soil ($q_{\text{demand}}$). This pressure induces a bending moment in the plate. The actual bending stress ($\sigma_{\text{bending}}$) is calculated using standard formulas for a cantilever plate under uniform load [2]. This stress must not exceed the allowable material stress ($\sigma_{\text{allowable}}$). Based on steel design standards like AISC 360, the allowable stress for serviceability conditions is often taken as a percentage of the steel's yield strength ($f_y$). A commonly used value for scaffolding or temporary structures is $0.75 \times f_y$, which provides an additional factor of safety [3].

    Jarak Kantilever: $c = \frac{b_{\text{plate}} - b_{\text{track}}}{2}$
    Cantilever Length: $c = \frac{b_{\text{plate}} - b_{\text{track}}}{2}$
    Tegangan Lentur Aktual: $\sigma_{\text{bending}} = \frac{3 \times q_{\text{demand}} \times c^2}{t_{\text{plate}}^2}$
    Actual Bending Stress: $\sigma_{\text{bending}} = \frac{3 \times q_{\text{demand}} \times c^2}{t_{\text{plate}}^2}$
    Tegangan Lentur Izin: $\sigma_{\text{allowable}} = 0.75 \times f_y$ Allowable Bending Stress: $\sigma_{\text{allowable}} = 0.75 \times f_y$

    Syarat Aman: $\sigma_{\text{bending}} \le \sigma_{\text{allowable}}$ Safety Condition: $\sigma_{\text{bending}} \le \sigma_{\text{allowable}}$

    b. Verifikasi Geser Pons (Punching Shear) b. Punching Shear Verification

    Verifikasi ini memastikan track tidak "menusuk" atau "merobek" steel plate. Tegangan geser ($\tau_{\text{punch}}$) dihitung pada keliling perimeter kritis di sekitar area track. Gaya geser netto adalah beban dari track dikurangi gaya dukung tanah yang bekerja di bawah area track itu sendiri. Tegangan geser aktual tidak boleh melampaui tegangan geser izin ($\tau_{\text{allowable}}$). Sesuai dengan spesifikasi AISC 360, tegangan geser izin dapat diambil sebesar 40% dari kuat leleh baja ($0.40 \times f_y$) untuk kondisi plastis [3, 4]. This verification ensures the track does not "punch" through or tear the steel plate. The shear stress ($\tau_{\text{punch}}$) is calculated along a critical perimeter around the track area. The net shear force is the track load minus the soil-bearing force acting under the track itself. The actual shear stress must not exceed the allowable shear stress ($\tau_{\text{allowable}}$). In accordance with AISC 360 specifications, the allowable shear stress can be taken as 40% of the steel's yield strength ($0.40 \times f_y$) for plastic conditions [3, 4].

    Gaya Pons Netto: $V = (\frac{\gamma_F \times W \times g}{2}) - (q_{\text{demand}} \times b_{\text{track}} \times L_{\text{contact}})$
    Net Punching Force: $V = (\frac{\gamma_F \times W \times g}{2}) - (q_{\text{demand}} \times b_{\text{track}} \times L_{\text{contact}})$
    Tegangan Geser Aktual: $\tau_{\text{punch}} = \frac{V}{2 \times (L_{\text{contact}} + b_{\text{track}}) \times t_{\text{plate}}}$
    Actual Shear Stress: $\tau_{\text{punch}} = \frac{V}{2 \times (L_{\text{contact}} + b_{\text{track}}) \times t_{\text{plate}}}$
    Tegangan Geser Izin: $\tau_{\text{allowable}} = 0.40 \times f_y$ Allowable Shear Stress: $\tau_{\text{allowable}} = 0.40 \times f_y$

    Syarat Aman: $\tau_{\text{punch}} \le \tau_{\text{allowable}}$ Safety Condition: $\tau_{\text{punch}} \le \tau_{\text{allowable}}$
